Waffles Recipe

This easy buttermilk waffle recipe is fast, flexible and fun

There’s a lot to be said for no-fuss, back-pocket recipes you can whip up any time. They can be especially helpfli come morning when we’re still rubbing the sleep out of our eyes.

These Buttermilk Waffles are exactly that kind of dish. The recipe is one I’ve been making for well over a decade, adapted from one of the first cookbooks I ever owned, “The Williams-Sonoma Bride & Groom Cookbook,” by Gayle Pirie and John Clark (a wedding present, of course).

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Add Milk to Bowl
  2. Add Eggs to Milk
  3. Add Sugar
  4. Wisk throughly
  5. Put in waffle maker
