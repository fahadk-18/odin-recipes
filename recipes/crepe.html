Crepes

Crepes are made with a simple mixture of ingredients that you probably already have on hand! Take these steps one at a time. It’s easier than you think!

Crepes are a versatile and fancy breakfast, brunch, dinner or dessert but couldn’t be easier. With this tried and true Crepe Recipe, you can make these at home with simple pantry ingredients in just a few minutes with no special cooking equipment required. Enjoy savory or sweet; crepes are the ultimate customizable meal.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Add Milk to Bowl
  2. Add Eggs to Milk
  3. Add Sugar
  4. Wisk throughly
  5. Put in waffle maker
